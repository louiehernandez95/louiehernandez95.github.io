Correlation Lesson

When two Pokémon stats move together

Correlation means we are looking for a pattern between two numbers. If heavier Pokémon often have higher attack, those two numbers are moving together. It is like noticing that the louder the cafeteria gets, the harder it is to hear your friend explain homework.

Correlation Is Not Causation

Patterns do not boss the universe around

If heavier Pokémon often have more attack, weight did not magically press an "attack goes up" button. Maybe evolution stage, type, game design, or legendary status is part of the story too.

A good data detective says: "I found a pattern. Now I need more clues before I say what caused it."
