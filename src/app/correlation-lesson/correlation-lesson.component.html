Correlation: when two things move together

Correlation is a number from -1 to 1. A positive number means two things often move together, a negative number means they often move in opposite directions, and 0 means there is not much pattern.

Try Saying It

Correlation does not mean cause

If two stocks rise on the same day, it does not prove one stock made the other rise. They might both be reacting to the same news, the same economy, or the same investor mood.

A good statistician says: "These things are connected in the data. Now we need more evidence before we claim one thing caused the other."
