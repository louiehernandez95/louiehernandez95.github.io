Do two things move together?

Imagine two classmates jumping rope. Sometimes they jump at the same time. Sometimes one jumps while the other stops. Correlation is just a pattern-spotting tool that asks: "Do these two things usually move together?"

Try Saying It

Correlation does not mean cause

If two stocks rise on the same day, that does not prove one stock made the other rise. They might both be reacting to the same news, the same economy, or the same investor mood.

A good data detective says: "I found a pattern. Now I need more clues before I say what caused it."
